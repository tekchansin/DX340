MAINTENANCE & RECOVERY

How to Uninstall

Change Boot profile to "Off". In Magisk, tap "Uninstall Magisk". The DX340 will reboot and be unrooted.

Soft Brick Recovery

Hold Bottom Side Button + Power. Release Power on Logo. Select "Recovery Mode" > "Wipe data/factory reset".

Hard Brick (QFIL)

DX340 uses Qualcomm SoC. Flash firmware via QFIL on Windows using the official factory image.

Modding Risks & Safety

Virtually no risk of a permanent brick. iBasso provides official images (.img) for QFIL recovery. As long as no physical damage occurs, software is 100% recoverable. Always keep a copy of the official Qualcomm image on your PC.
